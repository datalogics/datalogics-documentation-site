APDFL v18.0.5PlusP2a 

 

New Features: 

API updates to facilitate generation of ETSI PAdES-EPES (policy-based) signatures. 

This allows developers to define and include signature policy identifiers and associated policy information during signature creation, in compliance with ETSI PAdES specifications. 

 

Constraints: 

ETSI PAdES signatures are unavailable on Linux 32-bit and AIX 64-bit platforms.
